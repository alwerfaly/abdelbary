My academic path is shaped by a steady interest in how theory meets practice. At present I am especially engaged with artificial intelligence research, following advances that connect machine learning with communications, signal and image processing, and dependable engineering systems. In parallel, I devote attention to web design, crafting interfaces that are clear, purposeful, and respectful of the people who use them. I keep pace with emerging ideas across journals, open-source communities, and design standards, so that teaching, collaboration, and personal projects all reflect contemporary methods rather than yesterday’s defaults. The theses listed below summarise formal degree research; what continues to motivate me is the blend of rigorous inquiry, intelligent tools, and well-built digital experiences.
